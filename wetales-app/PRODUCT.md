## Brand Personality

**Reflective, editorial, intimate.**

Like a quiet corner in a well-curated gallery ‚ÄĒ not a chatbot, not a survey, not therapy. The brand evokes the feeling of sitting down with someone who genuinely wants to hear your story. Typography-forward, white-space-rich, deliberately unhurried.

## Anti-references

- **Not a chatbot**: Avoid ChatGPT/Claude's cold Q&A feel. No "I can help you with..." pleasantries.
- **Not a survey**: Avoid mechanical form-filling. This is a conversation, not a questionnaire.
- **Not therapy**: Avoid clinical, overly empathetic tones. No "how does that make you feel?"
- **Not SaaS**: Avoid dashboard aesthetics, metric cards, gradient CTAs.

## Design Principles

1. **Typography as atmosphere**: The type system carries the brand. Playfair Display for editorial gravitas, Inter for clarity, Noto Serif SC for Chinese warmth.
2. **Content is the interface**: Visual design serves the conversation and the article. Never compete with the story.
3. **Mobile-first intimacy**: The phone experience is primary ‚ÄĒ it's where people open up.
4. **Editorial restraint**: White space, deliberate hierarchy, no decorative flourishes. The magazine page should feel like it belongs in Monocle or The Gentlewoman.
5. **Personality through curation**: The choice of interviewer (Ś≥įŚď• vs ť≤ĀŤĪę) is the first creative decision. The homepage should make this choice feel meaningful, not transactional.
